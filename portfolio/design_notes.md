# Portfolio Design Notes & Layout Recommendations

## Webpage Layout Suggestions

Based on best practices for online resumes, the portfolio section should be visually engaging and easy to digest.

- **Placement:** Positioned prominently, typically right after "Experience" or "Skills."
- **Structure:** A Grid or Card-style layout is recommended. Each card should contain:
  - **Project Title:** Clear and descriptive.
  - **Context & Problem:** A 1-2 sentence summary of what the project solves or explores.
  - **Tech Stack:** A list of badges or tags (e.g., `Python`, `Pandas`, `Scikit-Learn`).
  - **Outcomes/Impact:** The measurable result or key takeaway (e.g., "Identified a 15% efficiency gain").
  - **Links:** Direct links to the code, live demo, or detailed case study.
